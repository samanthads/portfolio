Ski Helmet Safety Project

Technical Writing is a required upper-level course for all students in the Engineering School at Washington University in St. Louis. The final project for this course is a research paper and presentation on any technical subject of our choosing. As a former ski instructor, I thought it would be fun to research ski helmet safety. I researched the physics of helmets and head injuries, facts and statistics of head injuries from skiing and snowboarding, and any existing legislation mandating helmet use.
